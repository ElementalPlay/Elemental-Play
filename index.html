I am Erika Worman sole proprietor of Elemental Play where I taught and created pottery in Eugene for 25 years. My passion for ceramics began at Lane Community College in 1992 where a wheel throwing class opened me to a passion that will last a lifetime. I am a third generation Oregonian and I recently returned home to the Newport area after raising two beautiful children in Eugene. As a family we enjoyed time in nature hiking and camping which ignited an interest in mushrooms and all things forrest. Inspired by the magic of mushrooms I started hand sculpting mushroom and tree fairy houses on altered thrown pots. Spent any free time and money traveling the world with kids in tow. One of the many things l learned traveling is people love little light weight affordable souvenirs made LOCALLY to tuck in their suitcases. So my idea began to shrink my mushrooms and trees down to a size that fits on refrigerator magnets combine that with my love of my home state and here we are today.
